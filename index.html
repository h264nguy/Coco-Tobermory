Our Story

From Coco shifts to unforgettable memories.

Working at Coco is how all of us first got to know each other. What started as coworkers seeing each other during shifts slowly became real friendships, one person at a time. From busy workdays, small conversations, jokes, and after-work plans, we became closer and closer as a group.

This trip is special because it feels like the best and possibly last year where all of us can hang out together like this. Everyone may move on to different paths, but these memories will always remind us of the time we shared, the laughs we had, and the friendship we built together.
